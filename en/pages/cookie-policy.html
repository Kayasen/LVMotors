Cookie Policy

Information about cookies, external links and technologies used on the website.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small information files that may be downloaded to the user’s device when visiting a website.

What are cookies used for?

Cookies help the website remember information about the user’s visit, such as preferences or technical settings, making navigation easier and improving the user experience.

What type of cookies do we use?

At present, the LVMOTORS website does not use analytics, advertising, marketing, remarketing, Google Analytics, Meta Pixel or TikTok Pixel cookies.

Only technical or strictly necessary cookies may be used if essential to ensure navigation, security or basic website operation.

Session cookies and persistent cookies

Session cookies are temporary and remain in the browser only during the visit to the website. Persistent cookies may remain stored for a longer period, depending on their purpose.

Ads, Google Analytics and pixels

This website does not currently use advertising tools, Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, Meta Pixel, TikTok Pixel, Google AdSense, DoubleClick DART or similar technologies.

If statistics, advertising, conversion measurement or remarketing tools are implemented in the future, this Cookie Policy will be updated and an appropriate consent mechanism will be implemented before those cookies are activated.

External links

The website contains external links, including WhatsApp, Google Maps, Instagram, TikTok, Livro de Reclamações and other entities. These services are only opened when the user voluntarily clicks the respective links.

Managing cookies in the browser

The user can manage, block or delete cookies through the browser settings. Disabling certain technical cookies may affect the normal operation of the website.

Updates

LVMOTORS may update this Cookie Policy whenever relevant technical, legal or operational changes occur.
