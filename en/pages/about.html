About Us

At LVMOTORS, we believe that a car is much more than a means of transport, it is an extension of each client's personality, passion and lifestyle.

Driven by a vision of excellence, we work every day to offer a selection of selected vehicles, premium, sports and proven-quality models, carefully chosen based on their quality, history and exclusivity.

LVMOTORS was born with the aim of creating a distinctive automotive experience in Portugal, combining transparency, rigour and personalised support in every detail of the process.

With decades of experience in the automotive sector, our team combines technical knowledge, strategic vision and a genuine passion for the automotive world, ensuring a close, professional service aligned with the highest standards of quality.

More than selling cars, we seek to build lasting relationships with our clients, offering trust, discretion and continuous support before and after the vehicle is delivered.

Each car represents LVMOTORS' commitment to excellence, elegance and performance.

The Team

Meet the people behind LVMOTORS.

Luís Vieira

Founder & CEO

Passionate about supercars and driven by a vision of excellence, Luís Vieira is the founder and CEO of LVMOTORS, a car dealership created with a clear purpose: bringing a truly premium and exclusive concept to the market.

With a sharp eye for detail and an unwavering commitment to quality, Luís envisioned LVMOTORS as more than a place to buy cars, creating a luxury and confidence-driven experience for true automotive enthusiasts.

More than selling cars, he seeks to build lasting relationships with clients through personalised, transparent service aligned with the values of exclusivity, performance and elegance.

Pedro Lago Vieira

Rally Driver | Entrepreneur | Mechanical Engineer

With more than 12 years of experience in motorsport, Pedro Lago Vieira has built a career marked by competitiveness, dedication and passion for cars.

A rally driver, entrepreneur and mechanical engineer, he combines the technical side of engineering with a strategic vision of the automotive sector and the world of high performance.

Throughout his career, he has stood out in competition with titles such as National Junior Rally Champion and Portugal Rally Series Champion.

His vision for LVMOTORS is to create a distinctive brand built on exclusivity, emotion and genuine automotive passion.

Angelo Baleiro

Purchasing Director

With more than 30 years of experience in the automotive sector, Angelo Baleiro is a recognised reference in vehicle sourcing and careful selection.

He currently serves as Purchasing Director at LVMOTORS, where he brings his know-how, strategic vision and deep market knowledge to the brand.

Over the course of three decades, he has built a solid reputation based on rigour, trust and the ability to identify unique opportunities in both the national and international markets.
