About us

At LVMOTORS, we believe a car is far more than transportation — it is an extension of each client’s personality, passion and lifestyle.

Driven by a vision of excellence, we work daily to offer an exclusive selection of premium vehicles, supercars, special classics and high-performance models, carefully chosen for their quality, history and exclusivity.

LVMOTORS was created to deliver a distinctive automotive experience in Portugal, combining transparency, precision and personalised guidance throughout every step of the process.

With decades of experience in the automotive sector, our team combines technical expertise, strategic vision and a genuine passion for the automotive world, ensuring a close, professional service aligned with the highest standards of quality.

More than selling vehicles, we aim to build lasting relationships with our clients, offering trust, discretion and continuous support before and after delivery.

Every vehicle represents LVMOTORS’ commitment to excellence, elegance and performance.
