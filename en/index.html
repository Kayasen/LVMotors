About us

At LVMOTORS, we believe that the automobile is much more than a means of transport; it is an extension of each client’s personality, passion and lifestyle.

Driven by a vision of excellence, we work every day to offer a selection of carefully selected vehicles, premium and performance models of proven quality, carefully chosen based on their quality, history and exclusivity.

LVMOTORS was created with the aim of delivering a distinctive automotive experience in Portugal, combining transparency, precision and personalised support in every detail of the process.

With decades of experience in the automotive sector, our team combines technical knowledge, strategic vision and a genuine passion for the automotive world, ensuring a close, professional service aligned with the highest quality standards.

More than selling cars, we seek to build lasting relationships with our clients, offering trust, discretion and continuous support before and after the delivery of the vehicle.

Each car represents LVMOTORS’ commitment to excellence, elegance and performance.
